
 Summary of Learnings
There's just no shortcut for the deep understanding you get from working through problems yourself.This journey showed me how important it is to balance AI with building my own skills. AI can also speed things up in a space of time

 Main Insight
Ai is the best way to make my own learning louder and clearer, not to have it do the talking for me. before i use it, means  trying it first on my own, then asking it for guidance not answers and making sure I can turn around and explain it all back in my own ways.

Future Application
In future quests, I plan to:
1. Always attempt problems independently first
2. Use AI for specific questions about my solutions, not for generating solutions
3. Regularly test my understanding by explaining concepts without AI assistance
4. Focus on building debugging skills and system thinking
5. Use AI to explore alternative approaches only after mastering my own
